# Introduction

Welcome to **Digitalforce** — your ultimate hub for AI-powered digital transformation!

With **KIVA**, you can automate keyword research by unifying GSC insights, third-party data, hidden gems, and ChatGPT-powered optimizations—all in one platform for seamless implementation.

## Create an Account and Add Your Team

Getting started with Digitalforce is simple. Follow these steps to set up your account and onboard your team:

### 1. Sign Up
- Visit **Digitalforce** and click on **Sign Up**.

![Sign Up Page](/images/image28.png)

### 2. Create an Account
- Enter your email and password or sign up using your Google account for a seamless experience.

![Create Account](/images/image25.png)

### 3. Set Up Your Workspace

#### Workspace Name
- **Enter** Workspace name.

#### Description
- Add a **description** of your workspace.

#### Click "Next"
- Once added, proceed to **Next**.

![Workspace Setup](/images/image14.png)
